Why Linux?

  • Linux offers unmatched customization, letting you tailor every aspect from the kernel to the desktop without bloatware or restrictions.
  • It's free, open-source, and backed by a vast community, ensuring constant updates, tools, and support at zero cost.
  • Superior stability powers over 90% of the world's top servers, handling heavy loads without crashes.
  • Built-in security features like SELinux and rapid patching minimize vulnerabilities far better than proprietary OSes.
  • Perfect for developers with native support for scripting, containers, and virtualization in a lightweight, efficient environment.
